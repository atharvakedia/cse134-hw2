Extra Credit — MathML

Mass-energy equivalence, written with native <math> markup:

E=mc2

MathML has been around since the late 1990s, but Chrome didn’t get native support until Chrome 109 in January 2023 — Firefox and Safari had it for years before that. It’s a good reminder to check caniuse.com before leaning on a feature like this.
